Coppersplit Calculonicus

HTML5 Coin Split Calculator

Welcome! Please take a few minutes to read this help frame. Once you're done, click the "dismiss" link at the bottom, and it won't come up ever again. There are just a few steps you need to remember, so don't worry.

This application was made to help you split the plat across the guild, taking into account individual bonuses or penalties applied to guildies. Once you declare the total amount of coin, and the total number of members, you will need to type in the name of the members that have gained modifications in their split, either positive or negative. The application will keep tab on the global remainder and divide it among members without modifications.

This means, of course, that if you deduct plat from someone, this plat will be split among everyone else. Also, if you decide to give someone a bonus for whatever reason, the extra amount is taken collectively from everyone else.

As you can see by the example filled in below, the way you declare split modifications is pretty simple. You put in the name of the member followed by space-separated amounts, negative in this case. Each member needs to be on his own line. That's the only thing you need to remember.

The result pane on the right will be updated as you move around the fields. Additionally, you can click the "Calculate!" button at the bottom to force this to happen.
